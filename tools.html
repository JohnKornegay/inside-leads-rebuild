Common Questions

FAQ

Are these digital marketing tools really free?

Yes — every tool on this page has a free tier or is completely free with no credit card required. Some (like Moz or Ahrefs) offer premium upgrades, but the free versions provide genuine value for small businesses getting started with SEO or PPC.

Which free SEO tool should a small business start with?

Google Search Console first — always. It shows exactly which keywords drive traffic to your site, which pages are indexed, and any technical issues Google found. Set it up before any other tool. Then add Google Analytics 4 to see what visitors do after they arrive.

What's the best free tool for local SEO in Raleigh or Detroit?

Google Business Profile (completely free) is the most powerful local SEO tool available. Optimizing your GBP, posting weekly, and building reviews consistently through your review link will have more impact on local pack rankings than any paid tool.
